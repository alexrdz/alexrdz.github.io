Koala App

Koala is a web app built to help web developers in their job-seeking process. I worked with a team of 3 talented individuals on this full-stack MERN application (MongoDB, ExpressJS, ReactJS, and NodeJS).

View the App / View the Code

ReactJS, Node + ExpressJS, MongoDB, Webpack, Auth-0, Heroku, PostCSS

Role

Technical Lead & UI Developer

  • Initial set-up of Express Server and configuration

  • Configuration and deployment to Heroku
  • Github repository manager
  • UI/UX design and development (PostCSS & JSX)

User Flows & Wireframes

I led the team in a few exercises to determine user flows. We identified key areas where on which to focus and where pain points might arise. Because of the time limit (3 week project) we were couldn't do extensive user research. We conducted a couple of interviews to inform Koala's workflow process. I wireframed the app, conducted quick user testing, and proceeded to design and development.

Staying Organized is Key

Koala provides a way to not only find jobs but a way to manage the details throughout your process. We added a job search feature to take the hassle out of jumping around different sites to search for jobs. Users can save and remove an unlimited number of jobs, manage their process and notes, and stay organized during the crucial initial steps in the job-seeking process.

Keeping Notes

Notes are designed to help users keep track of the jobs in which they are most interested. From reminders of what day a user applied for a job, who the user spoke to on your phone, what they need to study before the interview, Koala helps keep things organized on a per-job basis.
